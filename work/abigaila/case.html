Abigaila

Abigaila (a play on the name Abigail Adams) was a project I started with a friend in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Upon being given access to a vast number of high quality textile SKUs, the product was designed to be one of the largest comprehensive online catalogs for home fabrics (curtains, covers, etc.). My work involved a product prototype, branding, a sales site, and a go-to-market strategy over the span of two weeks.
